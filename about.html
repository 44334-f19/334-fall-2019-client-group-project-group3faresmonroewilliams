About Us

Our Story

Our story start when we got our own Parti Yorki. We fell in love with her spunky personally and was a perfect fit for our home. Any time we brought Anna outside the home people would comment about her beautful unque color and that is when we decided that we wanted to share her future pups with other families. We take pride in raising our dogs as a true member of the family. We took great care to make sure that every puppy is in great health and is up to date on all of their shots and papers. We plan to continue to breed yoki's share their unique color to other families.

We want to make sure that our puppies go to truly good homes and that they grow up in a loving and safe enviornment. There will be an invterview process to ensure that each puppy goes to a home that is the perfect fit. All dogs come with their unique personalities and we work closly with our clients to ensure that each home is a perfect match!
